## 🔹 Project Overview
Analyze **customer purchasing behavior** using SQL and Python to uncover patterns in sales, product demand, and high-value customers.  
Goal: provide **actionable business insights** for marketing, sales, and customer retention strategies.

---

## 🔹 Dataset Description
The dataset includes:

| Column Name       | Description                          |
|------------------|--------------------------------------|
| Customer ID       | Unique identifier for each customer  |
| Product Category  | Category of purchased product        |
| Purchase Amount   | Amount spent per transaction         |
| Transaction Date  | Date of purchase                     |
| Region            | Customer region                      |

---

## 🔹 Tools & Technologies
- **SQL (SQLite)** – data querying and analysis  
- **Python** – execute queries, manage data  
- **Google Colab** – interactive environment  

---

## 🔹 Key Business Questions

1. Which product categories generate the highest revenue?  
2. Who are the top spending customers?  
3. What are the overall sales trends?  
4. Which regions contribute the most to sales?  
5. What are the most popular product categories?

---

## 🔹 SQL Concepts Demonstrated

- Filtering (`WHERE`)  
- Aggregation (`SUM`, `COUNT`, `AVG`)  
- Grouping (`GROUP BY`)  
- Sorting (`ORDER BY`)  
- Subqueries & analytical queries  

*Optional:* You can add window functions for extra impact (`ROW_NUMBER()`, `RANK()`).

---

## 🔹 Example Analysis & Insights

### Top Product Categories by Revenue
**Query Result:** Electronics generated the highest revenue.  
**Insight:** Electronics account for 38% of total revenue → focus marketing campaigns on high-demand products.

### High Value Customers
**Query Result:** Top 5 customers contributed 25% of total revenue.  
**Insight:** Retaining high-value customers is critical → consider loyalty programs.
